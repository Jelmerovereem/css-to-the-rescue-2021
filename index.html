Jonah's productivity setup

  • Task manager: Things 3
  • Calendar: Fantastical
  • Note taking: Craft Notes
  • Journal: Baronfig Confidant

I have quite a robust system consisting of four different pieces. I usually write three tasks in my journal that I do for the day, apart from the usual errands you typically run and other household tasks. I keep track of all tasks in Things 3, including household tasks. It keeps me on track with everything I care about. With my ADHD, it's easy to lose track of what I've done and become complacent.

I takes notes in an app called Craft Notes, a competitor to Notion. I prefer the way it organises my notes, so that's why I use it. For calendaring, I prefer Fantastical. It has natural language input support, which means I can write all the details of the event in a single sentence and get on my way quickly.
